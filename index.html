    How to play

    Climb a ladder of 5 trivia questions, one rung at a time. Rung 1 is easy; rung 5 is hard.

    • You get one typed guess (worth 3 points) and one multiple-choice pick. Type it right for the full 3 — or skip straight to the 4 choices for a max of 2. Miss your typed guess and the choices appear worth 1. Wrong pick = rung missed, but you keep climbing.
    • Typed it — 3 pts ·  picked it — 1–2 pts ·  missed.
    • Every rung comes from a different category — but all five answers secretly connect to one hidden theme.
    • At the top, you get one guess at the theme: +3 bonus points and a purple square if you crack it. Max score 18.

    One ladder per day, the same for everyone. Come back tomorrow to keep your streak alive.
